Getting started in 4 steps

Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Edge" above
The browser asks for confirmation — just click Add extension. No sign-in, no setup. TabLens is active immediately.
Press Alt+M from any tab
This opens TabLens with the search bar already focused — no need to reach for the mouse. You can also click the TabLens icon in your browser toolbar.
Type to find a tab, press Enter to switch
Results filter as you type. Use ↑ ↓ to move between results. Press / at any time to jump back to the search bar.
Browse normally — duplicate detection runs in the background
When you open a page that's already open, a small banner appears. Pick Continue or Take me there — it takes less than a second.

Your data stays on your device. TabLens cannot read the content of any page you visit. It never connects to a server, sends analytics, or requires an account. Everything runs locally in your browser.
