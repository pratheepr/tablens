Getting started in 4 steps

Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Edge" above
Confirm when the browser asks. No sign-in, no setup — TabLens is active immediately.
Press Alt+M from any tab
Opens TabLens with the search bar already focused. Or click the icon in the toolbar.
Type to find a tab, press Enter to switch
Results filter as you type. Use ↑ ↓ to navigate. Press / to jump back to search.
Browse normally — duplicates are caught in the background
If you open a page already open somewhere, a banner appears instantly. Pick Take me there to jump to it, or Continue to dismiss.

Your data stays on your device. TabLens cannot read the content of pages you visit. No server, no analytics, no account — everything runs locally in your browser.
